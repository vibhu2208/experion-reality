Experion Nova at Windchants - Sector 112 Gurgaon

Project Overview

A landmark in luxury living, Windchants spans over 24 acres of lush landscapes in Dwarka Expressway. It is the only residential project in NCR to get featured on National Geographic's Superstructures, showcasing its architectural brilliance. Home to one of Asia's largest residential skywalks, Windchants redefines exclusivity with one of the lowest densities in the region.

This ultra-luxurious, one-of-a-kind project offers expansive homes, world-class amenities, and a lifestyle crafted for those who demand the best. Experion is a Gurugram-based real estate developer, 100% FDI-funded backed by Experion Holding Pte. Ltd., Singapore, the real estate investing arm of the $2.5 billion AT Holdings group of companies.

Experion Nova at Windchants in Sector 112 Gurgaon represents the pinnacle of luxury residential living, combining architectural excellence with premium amenities to create an unparalleled living experience for discerning homebuyers.
