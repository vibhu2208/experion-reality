Project Overview

The number 3 holds significant value in many aspects of life. It symbolizes balance, harmony and completeness — often considered the number of wholeness. From storytelling to design, three elements create a natural rhythm, making things memorable and impactful. In many cultures, 3 is seen as a symbol of unity, combining past, present, and future, or body, mind, and spirit. It's no wonder that 3 is so often associated with success, growth and resilience.

Experion The Trillion offers a comprehensive range of world-class amenities that cater to the diverse needs of its residents. The project features a luxurious clubhouse equipped with a fully functional gym, spa, and wellness center, promoting a healthy and rejuvenating lifestyle. The infinity-edge swimming pool and kids' pool provide a perfect spot for relaxation and leisure.

Each residence at Experion The Trillion is designed with meticulous attention to detail, offering spacious 4BHK layouts that blend contemporary architecture with thoughtful functionality. The project sets new standards in premium living with its strategic location on Sohna Road, providing excellent connectivity and convenience for its residents.
